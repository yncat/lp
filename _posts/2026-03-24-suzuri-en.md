[Marimokikaku](https://marimokikakugk.com/) is a limited liability company specializing in accessibility-related topics.

This time, a joint event was held by GMO Pepabo and Marimokikaku, featuring a public accessibility review of Suzuri, a service developed by GMO Pepabo.

I participated as an accessibility expert and as someone who uses these technologies firsthand, conducting the live review and joining the discussion with attendees.

I had done some prep work on Suzuri's search and other features before the event, but there were far more screen-reader-tricky spots than I expected — things like the "add to cart" screen and the order screen. We had a great time exploring them together with everyone in attendance. (That's genuinely meant as a compliment!!!!!!!!!!!!!)

I would like to express my gratitude to [magi](https://x.com/magi1125), who invited me by name, and to everyone else involved.
